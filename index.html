What is Candle Range Theory?

Candle Range Theory is a powerful trading approach that focuses on the size of candlesticks and the behavior of price within their range. By studying how candles expand, contract, and interact with support and resistance, traders can identify high-probability entries and exits. Unlike strategies that rely only on indicators, Candle Range Theory uses raw price action, making it more reliable in fast-moving markets like Gold and EUR/USD.
